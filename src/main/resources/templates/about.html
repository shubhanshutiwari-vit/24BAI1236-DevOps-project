Who we are

About the department

We manage tourism infrastructure, heritage conservation and visitor services across Bihar.

Our mission

Make Bihar's ancient heritage and riverfronts accessible, safe and sustainably managed for every visitor.

Reliable by design

This portal runs on a containerized, auto-scaling platform with continuous monitoring, so bookings and maps stay available during festival-season traffic like Chhath Puja.

Community first

Partnering with local homestays, guides and conservation groups across every district.

Milestones

Department timeline

Department established

Formed to conserve and promote Bihar's heritage and pilgrimage sites.

Buddhist Circuit formalised

Bodh Gaya, Nalanda, Rajgir and Vaishali linked as a signposted heritage route.

Digital booking platform launched

Unified attractions, hotels and permits into one portal.

High-availability infrastructure rollout

Automated deployments and 24/7 monitoring introduced ahead of Chhath Puja season.
