Sample plan

4-day Buddhist Circuit

Day 1 — Arrival in Bodh Gaya

Settle in near the Mahabodhi Temple, evening meditation walk under the Bodhi Tree.

Day 2 — Nalanda & Rajgir

Morning at the Nalanda ruins, afternoon hot springs and ropeway at Rajgir.

Day 3 — Vaishali

Visit the Ashokan Pillar and stupa where the Buddha delivered his last sermon.

Day 4 — Patna & Departure

Morning at Patna Sahib and the Ganga ghats, onward journey by afternoon.
