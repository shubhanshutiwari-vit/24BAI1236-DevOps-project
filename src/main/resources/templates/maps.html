Getting there

By air, rail or road

By Air

Jay Prakash Narayan Airport, Patna

Daily flights from major metros; Gaya Airport also serves the Buddhist circuit directly.

By Rail

Patna & Gaya Junctions

Well-connected on the main line, with direct trains to Nalanda and Rajgir.

By Road

NH19 & NH31

Well-maintained highways link Patna to the Buddhist circuit towns.
